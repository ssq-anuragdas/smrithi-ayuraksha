Peace of Mind

Ayuraksha clinic has all types traditional massages which gives you relief and refreshment. Ayuraksha is providing treatments based on the traditional Ayurvedic knowledge.

PIZHICHIL

Warm medicated oil is poured over the body in continuous stream. It is very highly beneficial in rheumatic diseases like Arthritis, Paralysis, Hemiplegia, Nervous Weakness and Nervous Disorders. It rejuvenates and increases muscle tone.

KIZHI

Njavara is special variety of rice. It is cooked in medicinal decoction with milk into a pudding which is made to boluses tied up in muslin bag. It is very effective in Rheumatic complaints and muscle wasting.

ELAKIZHI

Leaves of medicinal plants along with powdered herbs are fried in medicated oil. They are tied in cotton cloths in the shape of bundles. These are applied over the body. It is useful in, Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid Arthritis, Spondylitis, Muscular Pain and Increases Muscle Tone.

THAKRA DHARA

Buttermilk mixed with a medical decoction is made to flow over the forehead. It is very effective in Insomnia, Hypertension, migraines, and Stress related Ailments.

DHANYAMLA DHARA

A specially prepared decoction is poured over the body. It is very effective in Painful Joints, Paralysis, and other Rheumatic Complaints.

NASYAM

Application of a medicated oil or juices of medicated plants through the nasal cavity. It is good for Headache, Sinusitis, Mental disorders and improves Eye sight.

KATI VASTI

Kati Vasthi is excellent for all types Back pain and Spinal disorders. It is the process of keeping specially prepared warm medicated oil over the lower back of the body marked with herbal paste boundary.

UDVARTHANAM

Fine medicated powder is applied over the whole body with upward strokes of massage, it is a special type of massage removes Fat from the Body, and Improves Blood Circulation. It is a whole-body massage with special herbal oils. It helps in Relaxing Mind and Body, Relieves Body Pain, Improves Complexion and Induces Sound Sleep.

SNEHAPANAM

Oral intake of medicated oils or ghee preparations is called Snehapanam. It is both a purification process and therapeutic procedure. Snehapana is done for Osteoarthritis, Psoriasis, Chronic constipation, etc.

PODIKIZHI

Podikizhi is stamping and massaging the body, whole or a specific affected part, using pouches containing powdered herbs. Podikizhi is an excellent anti-inflammatory therapy. Podikizhi is used for relieving pain, stiffness, cramps, swelling and inflammation. It also enhances circulation and opens the micro channels.

VIRECHANAM

Virechanam or purgation is induced by medicines and eliminate accumulated toxins from the alimentary canal, mainly from the small intestine. This procedure helps in eliminating Pitta Dosha.

THALAM

Herbal powder mixed with medicated oil is applied on the top of the head for 20 to 45 minutes. It is done for curing Migraine, Insomnia, ENT problems etc.

LEPANAM

It is the process of applying medicated herbal paste on the affected area especially in the cases of inflammatory disorders, Skin diseases, Arthritis etc.

ABHYANGAM

Abhyangam is a special type of oil massage. It is done with systematic and rhythmic strokes and pressures to the circulatory channels of the body. It is useful for Obesity, Sleeplessness, Fatigue, Body pain etc. It rejuvenates and refreshes both body and senses.

SIRO PICHU

Application of cotton soaked with medicated oil over the head. It cures Insomnia, Hair Fall and Migraine.
