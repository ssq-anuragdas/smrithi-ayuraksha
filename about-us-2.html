Dr. Smrithi, BAMS, MD ( Ayu) - The wound healing specialist

When we approach life from all facets of being — physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual – holistic healing works. Because change occurs on all levels, a holistic approach is seen to be more effective because good changes have a higher likelihood of lasting over the long term and enhancing a person's entire quality of life.

Holistic Wound Healing is an integrative type of therapy that incorporates both traditional and non-traditional treatments to help the individual as a whole.

Holistic Wound Healing tries to assist people in healing and thriving on a deeper level by taking into account emotional, physical, and spiritual variables that influence a person's well-being as opposed to concentrating on a specific issue.
